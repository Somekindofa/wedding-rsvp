🛂 Travelling from Turkey?

Turkish citizens need a Schengen visa to enter France. Appointment slots fill up fast, especially in Istanbul and Ankara, so we recommend starting the process at least 2 months before travel. Once you have an appointment, processing is usually under 2 weeks. You can apply via france-visas.gouv.fr or through a VFS Global centre in Ankara, Istanbul, Izmir or Gaziantep.
